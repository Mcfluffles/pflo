Simple, standardized freight pricing for reliable Prosperous Universe logistics. Customers purchase Standard Freight Units; PFLO handles routing, consolidation, and vessel assignment.

Standard Freight Unit

One Standard Freight Unit, or SFU, covers cargo up to both capacity limits below.

6,000 CIS / SFU

Fuel is charged separately by route.

Maximum Weight500 t
Maximum Volume500 m³
Minimum Shipment1 SFU

Quote Formula

Use the larger of weight or volume requirements to determine SFUs.

Freight = SFUs × 6,000 CIS
Total = Freight + Fuel Surcharge

Additional SFUs are required whenever either weight or volume exceeds the capacity of the current SFU count.

Fuel Policy

Fuel is charged separately from the SFU freight rate. This keeps the service rate stable while allowing PFLO to adjust fuel charges as market or production conditions change.

Fuel Surcharge = Route Fuel Requirement × Current Fuel Rate

Operating Terms

PFLO may consolidate multiple SFUs aboard a single vessel or split them across multiple vessels at its discretion. Pricing is based on Standard Freight Units purchased, not on the vessel assigned.

Availability, timing, and route acceptance are subject to fleet capacity.
